About

Hey, I’m Tung Quach

Software engineer from Vietnam. I mostly do backend and cloud stuff - Go, distributed systems, and all the unglamorous plumbing that keeps services alive. I like code that’s easy to read and maintain, not just code that happens to work.

How I got here

Started out taking things apart to see how they worked. Somewhere along the way that turned into a career - building backends, APIs, and the infra around them. I’ve touched the whole stack, but I’m happiest near the server.

Why I write here

  • To remember - I write down problems I’ve solved so I don’t have to solve them twice (and maybe save you the trouble too).
  • To learn - Nothing exposes the gaps in my understanding faster than trying to explain something.
  • To practice English - Not my first language, so writing regularly keeps it sharp.

Off the clock

When I’m not coding, I’m probably hunting for good coffee or messing with yet another side project.
